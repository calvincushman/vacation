Travel tips!

DOs:

  • stay hydrated
  • watch your pockets!
  • visit the temples (but not all of them)
  • learn at least SOME phrases in Thai

DON'Ts:

  • show people the bottoms of your feet
  • try to stand in line
  • talk about the king
  • try and remember Bangkoks REAL name (Krung Thep Mahanakhon Amon Rattanakosin Mahinthara Ayuthaya Mahadilok Phop Noppharat Ratchathani Burirom Udomratchaniwet Mahasathan Amon Piman Awatan Sathit Sakkathattiya Witsanukam Prasit)
